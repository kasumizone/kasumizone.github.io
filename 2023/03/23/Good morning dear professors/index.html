Good morning dear professors

Good morning/afternoon dear professors!

Thank you for giving me the opportunity for this interview.

My name is Liu Yurui, 23 years old ,and I come from Sicuan ,I graduated from Chengdu University of Information Technology in 2022(two thousand and twenty two) with a major in Network engineering.

During my college years, I passed CET-4, and I became a member of the Young Volunteers Association, and organized and participated in many volunteer activities.

After graduation, I worded as a Software Engineer in Chengdu.

I chose to apply for a master’s degree because after entering the workplace, I realized that there is still a lot of room for improvement in my professional konwledge and skills.

I know it is not easy to balance the exam-preparation and my work, and the preparation process was very difficult due to some force majeure. But I persevered and ended up here. I know that none of this has come easily, All the more reason for me to seize this opportunity.

Next, I want to talk about my future plans.

I will take every course in graduate school seriously, and read vast relevant literature to form a more systematic view of Software Engineering.

Baside that, I will concentrate on the study on my porfessional field and work hard to improve my research ability.

As you can see, I’m the kind of person who has to go all out for whatever I do. If I failed, I could accept it. But I knew that if I did things halfheartedly and they didn’t work out, I’d always have regrets. So, I will try my best to use critical and independent thinking in my studies.

That’s all about me. If I am lucky enough to be admitted, I will devote myself to the study and research in the next two and a half years. Thanks for your time, have a nice day.
